## Executive Summary

This document outlines Phoenix Rooivalk's partnership strategy for expanding market reach, enhancing capabilities, and building ecosystem presence in the counter-UAS and DePIN markets.

### Tier 1: Strategic Partners

Long-term, deep integrations with revenue sharing and joint go-to-market.

| Partner Type | Value Exchange | Target Partners |

| Partner Type | Value Exchange | Target Partners |

| **Satellite** | Imagery + positioning | Planet Labs, Maxar, Starlink |

### Tier 3: Channel Partners

Distribution and go-to-market partnerships.

| Partner Type | Value Exchange | Target Partners |
|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **System Integrators** | Implementation + support | Local defense SIs |
| **Resellers** | Geographic reach | Regional security distributors |

## Go-to-Market Alignment

### Joint Marketing Activities

| Activity | Frequency | Investment |
|----------|-----------|------------|

| **Webinars** | Monthly | In-kind |

### Lead Sharing Protocol

1. **Registration**: Partners register opportunities in shared CRM
2. **Qualification**: Joint qualification call within 48 hours
3. **Assignment**: Clear ownership based on lead source
4. **Tracking**: Shared pipeline visibility
