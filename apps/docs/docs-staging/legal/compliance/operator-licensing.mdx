## Overview

This document outlines the licensing, certification, and regulatory requirements for Phoenix Rooivalk operators, third-party node operators, and DePIN network participants.

---

## Operator Categories

### Internal Operators

Phoenix Rooivalk employees operating company-owned equipment.

| Role | Requirements | Certification |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Drone Pilot** | RPL, medical, background check | Annual recertification |
| **System Operator** | Internal training, clearance | Quarterly assessment |
| **Field Technician** | Technical certification | Annual recertification |

### Partner Operators

Third-party organizations operating Phoenix Rooivalk equipment under agreement.

| Role | Requirements | Certification |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Authorized Operator** | Partnership agreement, training | Annual audit |
| **Reseller Technician** | Technical certification, NDA | Initial + updates |
| **Government Operator** | Contract, clearance verification | Per contract |

### DePIN Node Operators

Independent operators contributing to decentralized networks.

| Role | Requirements | Certification |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Data Node Operator** | Node agreement, KYC | Initial registration |
| **Sensor Contributor** | Device registration, terms acceptance | Automated |
| **Compute Provider** | Compute agreement, performance SLA | Quarterly review |

## South African Regulatory Requirements

### SACAA (Civil Aviation Authority)

| Requirement | Applicability | Details |
|-------------|--------------|---------|
| **Remote Pilot License (RPL)** | All drone operators | Part 101 compliance |
| **ROC (RPAS Operator Certificate)** | Commercial operations | Company-level certification |
| **Medical Certificate** | RPL holders | Class 4 or higher |
| **Flight Permissions** | Controlled airspace, BVLOS | Per-operation approval |

### SAPS & Intelligence Services

| Requirement | Applicability | Details |
|-------------|--------------|---------|
| **Security Clearance** | Defense-related operations | NIA vetting |
| **PSIRA Registration** | Security services | Category dependent |
| **Firearm Competency** | Armed response integration | Where applicable |

### NCACC (Defense Articles)

| Requirement | Applicability | Details |
|-------------|--------------|---------|
| **Registration** | Counter-UAS equipment | Equipment registration |
| **End-User Certificate** | Export/transfer | Per transaction |
| **Record Keeping** | All controlled items | 5+ year retention |

## Enforcement

### Violations

| Violation | Severity | Consequence |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| **Expired License** | Medium | Suspension until renewed |
| **Falsified Credentials** | Critical | Immediate termination |
| **SLA Breach (minor)** | Low | Warning |
| **SLA Breach (repeated)** | Medium | Tier demotion |
| **Data Quality Failure** | Medium | Earnings withheld |
| **Safety Incident** | Critical | Investigation, potential termination |

### Appeals Process

1. **Initial Review**: Operations manager (5 business days)
2. **Appeal**: Compliance committee (10 business days)
3. **Final Appeal**: Executive decision (15 business days)
