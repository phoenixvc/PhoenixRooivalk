## Terms of Use - Key Provisions

### Buyer Obligations

| Obligation | Description | Consequence of Breach |
|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Authorized Use** | Use data only for stated purpose | Account termination, legal action |
| **No Redistribution** | Cannot resell or share data | License revocation, damages |
| **Compliance** | Follow all applicable laws | Indemnification required |
| **Security** | Protect data from unauthorized access | Liability for breaches |
| **Reporting** | Report misuse or security incidents | Account suspension |

### Seller Obligations

| Obligation | Description | Consequence of Breach |
|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Data Rights** | Must own or have rights to sell | Removal, revenue clawback |
| **Accuracy** | Data must match description | Refunds, removal |
| **Compliance** | No illegal or restricted data | Account termination, legal action |
| **Support** | Respond to buyer inquiries | Rating impact, removal |
| **Updates** | Maintain data quality | Delisting |

## Data Licensing Models

### License Types

| License | Rights Granted | Restrictions | Typical Use |
|---------|---------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Single-Use** | One-time access | No storage, no derivative works | Verification queries |
| **Standard** | Limited use, internal only | No redistribution, attribution required | Analytics, research |
| **Extended** | Broad internal use | No resale, limited derivatives | Product development |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited internal use | Custom negotiation | Large organizations |
| **Reseller** | Redistribution rights | Revenue share, approval required | Partners |

### License Terms Matrix

| Term | Single-Use | Standard | Extended | Enterprise |
|------|------------|----------|----------|------------|
| **Duration** | Instant | 1 year | 1 year | Custom |
| **Users** | 1 | 10 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Storage** | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Derivatives** | No | Limited | Yes | Yes |
| **Attribution** | N/A | Required | Optional | Waived |
| **SLA** | None | 99% | 99.5% | 99.9% |

## Liability Framework

### Platform Liability

| Scenario | Phoenix Rooivalk Liability | Limitation |
|----------|---------------------------|------------|
| **Data Quality** | Limited to refund | Seller responsible for accuracy |
| **Payment Failures** | Full responsibility | Payment processing SLA |
| **Platform Downtime** | Pro-rata credit | Force majeure excluded |
| **Security Breach** | Depends on fault | Capped at fees paid |
| **Third-Party Claims** | Indemnification from sellers | Pass-through liability |

### Limitation of Liability

```
Maximum Liability Cap:
- For Buyers: Greater of (fees paid in last 12 months) or ($10,000)
- For Sellers: Greater of (revenue earned in last 12 months) or ($10,000)
- For Enterprise: As negotiated in MSA

Excluded from Cap:
- Intentional misconduct
- Gross negligence
- IP infringement (willful)
- Data protection violations
- Indemnification obligations
```

### Indemnification

| Party | Indemnifies Against |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Buyer** | Misuse of data, violation of license terms, third-party claims from use |
| **Seller** | IP infringement, inaccurate data, privacy violations, export control violations |
| **Platform** | Platform IP infringement, gross negligence in operations |

### Resolution Process

```
Step 1: Direct Resolution (5 business days)
    │

    │
```

## Regulatory Compliance

### Export Control

| Regulation | Applicability | Compliance Measure |
|------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **NCACC** | Defense-related data | Export permit required |
| **ITAR** | US defense technology | No export without license |
| **EAR** | Dual-use technology | Classification review |
| **EU Dual-Use** | EU customers | End-user screening |

### Data Protection

| Regulation | Trigger | Requirement |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **POPIA** | SA data subjects | Consent, processing records |
| **GDPR** | EU data subjects | DPA, SCCs for transfers |
| **CCPA** | California residents | Disclosure, opt-out rights |

### Financial Regulations

| Regulation | Applicability | Compliance Measure |
|------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **FICA** | SA AML requirements | KYC for high-value transactions |
| **FATF** | Crypto payments | Transaction monitoring |
| **Tax** | Revenue recognition | Withholding where required |

## Payment Terms

### x402 Payment Legal Framework

| Aspect | Treatment |
|--------|-----------|
| **Payment Finality** | Blockchain confirmation = payment complete |
| **Refunds** | Platform credit only (no on-chain reversal) |
| **Currency** | USDC (stablecoin) as primary |
| **Tax** | Buyer responsible for local tax obligations |
| **Chargebacks** | Not applicable (crypto payments) |

### Revenue Recognition

| Event | Recognition |
|-------|-------------|
| **Data Delivered** | Immediate recognition |
| **Subscription** | Pro-rata over term |
| **Failed Delivery** | Refund/credit |
