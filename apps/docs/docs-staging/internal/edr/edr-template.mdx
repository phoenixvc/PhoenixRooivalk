# EDR Template (Engineering Decision Record)

Use this template for new Engineering Decision Records. Save as
`YYYY-MM-DD-short-title.mdx` in this folder (e.g.
`2025-03-04-imu-standardization.mdx`).

---

## Title

One line: decision or topic (e.g. "IMU standardization for control and
navigation").

## Date

YYYY-MM-DD

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by [link]

## Context

- What problem or opportunity does this address?
- What constraints or options were considered?

## Decision

- What was decided? (1–3 short paragraphs or bullets.)

## Consequences

- **Positive** — Benefits and enablers.
- **Negative / Trade-offs** — Downsides, migration cost, or limitations.
- **Follow-ups** — Actions, owners, or future EDRs.
