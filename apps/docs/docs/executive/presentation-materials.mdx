## Technology Glossary (Presenter Background)

### Edge AI Terms

| Term | Definition | Why It Matters |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **YOLOv9** | "You Only Look Once" v9 - latest real-time object detection model (2024). mAP 95.7%, 4.6% improvement over YOLOv8. | State-of-the-art accuracy at inference speeds suitable for real-time drone detection |
| **mAP** | Mean Average Precision - standard metric for object detection accuracy (0-100%). Industry benchmark. | Our 95.7% vs competitors' 85-95% means fewer missed detections |
| **Jetson Orin** | NVIDIA's edge AI platform. AGX Orin delivers 275 TOPS (trillion operations/second). | Enables on-device inference without cloud latency |
| **TOPS** | Tera Operations Per Second - measure of AI compute capability. | 275 TOPS = can run complex models in real-time |
| **Edge inference** | Running AI models on-device rather than in cloud. | No network latency, works offline, GDPR compliant (data stays local) |
| **TensorRT** | NVIDIA's inference optimizer. 5-10x speedup over raw PyTorch. | How we achieve sub-200ms response times |

### Blockchain Terms

| Term | Definition | Why It Matters |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Solana** | High-performance L1 blockchain. 65K-100K TPS, 400ms finality, $0.00025/tx. | Only chain with speed + cost for continuous evidence logging |
| **TPS** | Transactions Per Second - blockchain throughput. Solana: 65K+, Ethereum: ~15. | We need high TPS for real-time event logging |
| **Finality** | Time until transaction is irreversible. Solana: 400ms, Ethereum: 12-15 min. | Evidence must be immutable quickly for legal validity |
| **Chain of custody** | Legal term for documented, unbroken evidence trail. | Blockchain provides tamper-proof, timestamped audit trail |
| **Ed25519** | Digital signature algorithm used by Solana. 256-bit security. | Cryptographic proof of who logged what and when |

### Counter-UAS Terms

| Term | Definition | Why It Matters |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **C-UAS** | Counter-Unmanned Aircraft Systems - technologies to detect, track, and defeat drones. | Our core enterprise market |
| **SORA** | Specific Operations Risk Assessment - EU drone regulation framework. | Required for commercial drone defense operations |
| **False positive rate** | Percentage of non-drone objects incorrectly identified as drones. Industry: 15-25%, ours: <0.3%. | False positives = costly false alarms, liability risk |
| **Response time** | Time from detection to action. Industry: 5-30s, ours: 120-195ms. | Faster response = higher intercept success |

### Swarm / Multi-Agent Terms

| Term | Definition | Why It Matters |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **MARL** | Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning - AI technique where multiple agents learn cooperative behaviors. | Enables coordinated multi-drone defense |
| **Swarm intelligence** | Collective behavior emerging from simple local rules (separation, alignment, cohesion). | Decentralized coordination without central control |
| **Decentralized consensus** | Agreement protocol without central authority. | Resilient to single-point failures |
