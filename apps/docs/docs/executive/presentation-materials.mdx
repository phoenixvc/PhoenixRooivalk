## Q&A Preparation

### "Why consumer before enterprise?"

Consumer market is faster to enter:
- Lower regulatory burden (CPSC vs FAA)
- D2C channel mature and cost-effective
- Revenue validates tech before enterprise investment
- Consumer funds enterprise R&D timeline

### "What's the competitive moat?"

1. **Speed**: {PERFORMANCE.RESPONSE_TIME} vs 5-30s competitors
2. **Cost**: In-house net manufacturing from existing Kevlar stock
3. **Evidence**: Only solution with {BLOCKCHAIN.PRIMARY_CHAIN} blockchain audit
4. **Dual revenue**: Consumer + Enterprise reduces market risk

### "Why now?"

- **November 2024 crisis**: Brussels 8-day closure, Poland 4 airports, Denmark drone ban
- **4x YoY increase** in European airport drone incidents
- **Edge AI maturity**: YOLOv9 + Jetson Orin enables real-time edge inference
- **Blockchain cost collapse**: {BLOCKCHAIN.PRIMARY_CHAIN} at {BLOCKCHAIN.COST_PER_TX}/tx makes continuous logging viable
- **Regulatory pressure**: EU and US mandating airport drone defense
