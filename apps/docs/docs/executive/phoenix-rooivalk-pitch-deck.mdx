## Presentation Tips

### Opening Strong

Start with video footage, then hit the DJI ban:

> _"This is happening right now. The FCC just banned DJI drones in December
> citing national security. Brussels Airport was closed twice in one night. And
> there's nothing affordable to protect your home. Until now."_

### Key Differentiators

Lead with what makes us unique:

- **10-150x faster** than competitors (sub-200ms vs 5-30 seconds)
- **Only** C-UAS with blockchain evidence chain
- **Only** consumer product in the market ($349)
- **Safe propellant** - no permits required

### The Ask

> _"$960K seed on a SAFE at $3.5M pre-money cap. 18-month runway to Series A
> with $500K+ ARR."_
