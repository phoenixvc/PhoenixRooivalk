## Overview

**Techstars** operates 40+ accelerator programs globally with various industry focuses. Several programs are directly relevant to Phoenix Rooivalk, including Air Force, Anywhere (remote), and industry-specific tracks.

:::info Key Details

- **Investment**: $120,000 standard
- **Equity**: 6% common stock
- **Duration**: 13-week programs
- **Network**: 3,600+ portfolio companies
- **Demo Days**: Global investor access
- **Website**: [techstars.com](https://www.techstars.com)

:::

## Application Process

### Step 1: Online Application
- Company overview (30 min)
- Team backgrounds
- Product description
- Traction metrics

### Step 2: First Interview
- 30-minute video call
- Founding team required
- Product/market fit discussion

### Step 3: Partner Interview
- 60-minute deep dive
- Technical discussion
- Investment terms review

### Step 4: Selection
- 2-4% acceptance rate
- Notification 4-6 weeks before start
- Terms negotiation if accepted
