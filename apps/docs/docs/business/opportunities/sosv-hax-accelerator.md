## Program Overview

**SOSV HAX** is the world's leading hardware accelerator, part of the SOSV
venture capital firm managing over $1.4 billion in assets. HAX specializes in
deep tech startups building physical products with a focus on robotics, health
devices, and industrial automation.

:::info Key Details

- **Investment**: $250,000 for 10% equity
- **Duration**: 4-month intensive program
- **Location**: Newark, NJ (with global presence in Shenzhen)
- **Focus Areas**: Robotics, Health, Industrial, Climate Tech
- **Application**: Rolling admissions
- **Website**: [hax.co](https://hax.co)

:::

## What HAX Provides

### Funding

- **$250,000 initial investment** (standard terms)
- Access to SOSV's $1.4B+ fund for follow-on
- Bridge financing between rounds

### Hardware Resources

- **Prototyping facilities** in Newark and Shenzhen
- Manufacturing partner introductions
- Bill of Materials (BOM) optimization
- Supply chain guidance

### Mentorship

- Weekly sessions with hardware experts
- Technical advisors in robotics, electronics, mechanical
- Go-to-market strategy for hardware products
- Regulatory and compliance guidance

### Network

- 400+ HAX alumni companies
- Corporate partnership introductions
- Investor network for Series A/B
- Demo Day exposure to 500+ investors
