# UK Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA)

The **Defence and Security Accelerator (DASA)** finds and funds exploitable
innovation to support UK defence and security. Part of UK Defence Innovation
(UKDI), DASA provides non-dilutive funding for defense technology development.

---

## Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Organizer** | UK Ministry of Defence |
| **Part of** | UK Defence Innovation (UKDI) |
| **Funding** | Up to £350K per proposal |
| **Focus** | Defence and security innovation |
| **Vision** | UK strategic advantage through innovation |

---

## NATO DIANA UK Partnership

DASA partners with Janus Consortium to deliver the **NATO DIANA** programme
in the UK.

### 2026 DIANA Cohort
- **Challenge:** Contested Electromagnetic Environments
- **Funding:** £100K+ contractual funding
- **Applications:** 3,680 submissions (top 4% accepted)
- **Test Facilities:** Access to 180+ facilities

**Eight companies selected for 2026 cohort** from highly competitive process.

## Application Process

1. **Review** current opportunities on GOV.UK
2. **Contact** Innovation Partner for guidance
3. **Submit** proposal through portal
4. **Assessment** by technical experts
5. **Decision** typically 6-8 weeks
6. **Contract** if successful
