# India iDEX - Defence India Startup Challenge

**Innovations for Defence Excellence (iDEX)** is India's flagship defense
innovation program, supporting startups developing cutting-edge military
technologies including UAS and autonomous systems.

---

## Program Overview

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Organizer** | Defence Innovation Organisation (DIO), Ministry of Defence |
| **Founded** | April 2018 |
| **Budget** | Rs. 498.78 crore (2021-2026) |
| **Contracts Signed** | 430+ (as of Feb 2025) |
| **Focus** | Defence and aerospace innovation |

---

## Funding Tracks

### DISC (Defence India Startup Challenge)
- **Grants:** Up to Rs. 1.5 Crore (~$180,000 USD)
- **Focus:** Specific problem statements from armed forces
- **Current Edition:** DISC 12 (41 problem statements)

### iDEX Prime
- **Grants:** Up to Rs. 10 Crore (~$1.2M USD)
- **Focus:** Higher-value technology development

### ADITI (Acing Development of Innovative Technologies)
- **Grants:** Up to Rs. 25 Crore (~$3M USD)
- **Focus:** High-value strategic technologies

## Current Challenges (DISC 12)

Check [idex.gov.in/challenges](https://idex.gov.in/challenges) for current
problem statements. Typical CUAS-relevant challenges include:

- Anti-drone systems for critical infrastructure
- Swarm detection and neutralization
- Low-cost counter-UAS solutions
- Mobile air defense systems

## Eligibility

| Requirement | Phoenix Rooivalk Status |
|-------------|------------------------|
| Startup/MSME | Meets requirement |
| Innovative solution | Meets requirement |

| Technology alignment | Strong alignment |

:::info International Participation

iDEX primarily supports Indian companies but may consider international
partnerships. Contact iDEX directly for foreign company participation options.

:::

---

## Application Process

1. **Register** on iDEX portal
2. **Review** current DISC challenges
3. **Submit** proposal aligned to challenge
4. **Evaluation** by technical committee
5. **Selection** for funding and mentorship
6. **Development** with milestone-based payments

## Phoenix Rooivalk Strategy

### Recommended Approach

1. **Identify Partner:** Find Indian defense startup for collaboration
2. **Joint Application:** Apply together for DISC challenge
3. **Technology Transfer:** License technology to Indian partner
4. **Local Manufacturing:** Support Make in India initiatives

### Key Considerations

- India has strong "Make in India" preference
- Partnership with Indian company highly recommended
- Counter-drone is high-priority area for Indian military
- Good fit for technology licensing model
